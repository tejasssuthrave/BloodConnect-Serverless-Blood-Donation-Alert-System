About The Platform

Built to reduce delay in emergency blood access

Blood Donation Alert System is a healthcare initiative focused on connecting hospitals with nearby verified donors during life-critical emergencies. Our goal is to reduce manual outreach time and improve coordination between donors, hospitals, and response teams.

Mission

Enable faster blood donor response for emergency care across cities.

Coverage

Used by hospital partners, community organizations, and volunteer networks.

Approach

Digital-first alerting, verified donor information, and operational clarity.

How It Works

1. Donor registers

Donors submit blood group, city, and contact details on the platform.

2. Hospital raises a request

Emergency teams submit requirement details and priority.

3. Alerts are dispatched

Eligible nearby donors are notified quickly for urgent support.

4. Critical care is supported

Matched donors reach hospitals faster and improve treatment timelines.
