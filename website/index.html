How It Works

1. Donor Registers

A donor joins the platform with blood group, city, and contact details.

2. Hospital Requests Blood

Hospital staff submit an emergency requirement with blood group and location.

3. Emergency Alerts Sent

The system is designed to trigger alerts to nearby eligible donors in minutes.

4. Donor Saves a Life

Matched donors reach the hospital quickly to support urgent transfusion needs.

Impact of Timely Blood Donation

Every minute matters during accidents, surgeries, and critical care. A connected blood donor network can reduce treatment delays and improve survival outcomes.
